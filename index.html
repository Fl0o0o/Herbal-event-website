About Herbal

Herbal is an underground music experience focused on blending hip hop and electronic music into a new form of sonic expression. Taking place in New York, Brooklyn at Club transpecos, the event creates a space for experimental sound, creative freedom, and immersive energy. It is designed for audiences who are looking beyond mainstream music and into the future of underground culture.

Westwood and Coreylingo bring a hybrid sound that merges underground hip hop with electronic production. Their sets are filled with distorted basslines, atmospheric textures, and unpredictable transitions. The performance moves between rhythm and chaos, creating a raw and emotional experience that reflects the evolution of modern underground music.

DJ sets by Flo and Samo focus on high-energy selections influenced by techno and hyperpop. Fast-paced mixing, heavy drops, and intense momentum define the night’s energy. Opening the event, Harbor introduces the atmosphere with a more ambient and building sound, preparing the crowd for the full experience of Herbal.
